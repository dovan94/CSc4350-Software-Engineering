Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long would it take for my order to arrive?
A: It wil usually take between 5-7 business days for standard shipping.

Q: Will I be able to refund my book if it wasn't the right one?
A: We allow refunds up to 30 days with everything that came along with the book intact.

Q: How do I check the status of my order?
A: You would have to go to Account > Orders > Track Order to see the status of your order and where it is located.

Q: What if I can't find the book I'm looking for?
A: Shoot us an email! We will order a book for you but it may take a little longer for you to recieve it in the mail due to us having to find the book for your needs.
